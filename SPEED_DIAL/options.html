How does it work?

Sync

With Speed Dial Pro, you bookmarks and settings are being automatically synchronized to our server speeddial2.com. This means your extension with all dials, groups and settings will be synchronized across different computers right after you provide you sync credentials (username and password). Synchronization with our server takes place every time you start a browser and any other changes (creating new bookmarks or group) are immediatelly replicated online.

Mobile / online access

Since your groups and bookmarks will be available online, you can access them from any other browser or mobile device by logging in at my.speeddial2.com. They're optimized to be viewed on mobile devices and tablets as well as desktop computers. To quickly access your bookmarks from your mobile device, set Speed Dial 2 as a homepage or pin it as a bookmark on your Homescreen.
